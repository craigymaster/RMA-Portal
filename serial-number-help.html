Where to find the serial number

The serial number uniquely identifies your terminal. Here's how to locate it.

1

Label on the back of the device

Turn the terminal over. On the back you will find a sticker with a barcode. The serial number is printed directly below the barcode and is labelled S/N or SN.

Example: PA1234567890123
2

Via the device settings

You can also find the serial number in the device menu:

  • Open Settings on the terminal
  • Go to About device or About phone
  • The serial number is listed under Serial number or SN
3

On the original packaging

The serial number is also printed on the barcode label of the original box the terminal came in, under the heading SN or Serial No.

Serial number prefixes per terminal type
Prefix Terminal type
PA…P3 Air
P3…P3H
PK…P3KH
PD…P2 SmartPads
PN…P2 Mini
P2…P2 Pro
PC…P2 Lite SE
TJ…P2 SE
PF…P3 Mix
The serial number is always 13 characters long and consists of letters and numbers. Make sure you enter the full number exactly as shown on the label.
